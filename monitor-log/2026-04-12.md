### New Skills Created (3)

1. **Student Feedback Generator** (`skills/operations/student-feedback-generator.md`)
   - Generates personalized formative feedback on student work
   - Identifies strengths, growth areas, and actionable next steps
   - Adjusts for grade level, accommodations, and rubric criteria
   - Estimated time saved: ~15 min/student

2. **Curriculum Standards Aligner** (`skills/operations/curriculum-standards-aligner.md`)
   - Maps lessons/units to official standards frameworks (Common Core, NGSS, state, IB, AP)
   - Produces alignment matrix with gap analysis
   - Flags coverage gaps and sequencing issues
   - Estimated time saved: ~45 min/unit

3. **Student Progress Report Writer** (`skills/admin/student-progress-report-writer.md`)
   - Converts raw gradebook data into narrative progress reports
   - Supports individual student narratives and class-wide trend summaries
   - Flags students for intervention or recognition
   - Estimated time saved: ~20 min/report
